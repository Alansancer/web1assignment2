Learning to create Lists in HTML

There are different kind of tags that we can use in HTML to help us better organzie our website.
There are different kind of tags, the first ones we are going to discuss are the ones that appear
we start our website by typing the exclamation mark (!).
Down bellow you can find a couple of examples:

Header
This tag is to add a title to your website, you can use the tags "h1 and h2."
This tags are given priority so they are always consider to be at the top
Body
The body tag is where most of the information of your website will be located.
Your body will contain "h3 to h6." This are headers that are not consider as important or relevant
Similarly, it will have "p" where it will dive your parragraphs and other text into different blocks.
Since, the nature of text will be to continue on the same line until the end of everything it is recommended you use "br"
This tag will help you make your text managable in your website
Footer
Finally, the footer is where you can add your copy right information. To do so
You can just add a "p" and after it you type "& copy;" all together
Imag
Here is where you put your image by using "img equals src" and afterwards a small description using alt
Navigation
To create links between your own created website you use nav, where you use ul to create a list
that will create a list of your other screens to make navigation between you creations easier.
